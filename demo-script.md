# 7-Minute Demo Script

1. BEL starts with a digital twin of a complex defence electronics system.
2. Live synthetic telemetry streams into the twin.
3. A mission scenario is triggered.
4. Thermal, structural, RF and EO surrogate models calculate system impact.
5. The platform predicts risk before physical failure.
6. Operator runs what-if mitigation and reduced-envelope decision support.
7. The digital thread captures evidence, simulation run IDs and validation status.
8. BEL retains source ownership, auditability and on-prem autonomy.
